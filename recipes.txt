Recipe Name: Honey Garlic Salmon Bites
Ingredients:
- Honey: 10.00
- Garlic: 5.00
- Salmon: 20.00
Instructions:
1. Cut salmon into bite sized pieces.
2. Mix honey and garlic in a bowl.
3. Coat salmon pieces with the mixture.
4. Cook in a pan until golden brown.
